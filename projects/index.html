Energy Disaggregation

At the University of California, Berkeley, I've been working on energy disaggregation. My tool, DisCoVA, improves on current benchmarks by 10-20% using variational autoencoders. I was inspired by their lower reconstruction loss vs. traditional autoencoders in classic image datasets.

The Brownlow Downlow

I've been working on predicting the MVP of the Australian Football League. I've used a balanced bagging approach to both combat class imbalance and generate "soft" votes with uncertainties. Using a random forest as the base model I predicted 5 of the top 7 players on 2015 test data.
