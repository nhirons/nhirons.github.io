Career Goals

I want to use machine learning to help solve challenging, high impact problems in industries like energy, health, education, infrastructure and agriculture. I have a strong bias towards application over research, but I try to learn and understand some of the latest developments with the help of the amazing people in Machine Learning @ Berkeley.

Interests

I love dogs; I have a blue heeler or "Autralian Cattle Dog" named Buster and I used to have a border collie named Mischief. My childhood and family revolved/revolves around sports, and I love basketball, playing all my life until four hip operations put me out for good! My close friends and family mean the world to me and I can't wait to visit them soon.

Food

I love food, especially brunch and dinner in my home city of Melbourne, Australia. Nothing beats sweet potato, zucchini and corn fritters, goat cheese, beetroot hummus, smashed avocado, poached eggs and maybe some bacon or candied prosciutto. Brighton Soul Espresso, Little Ox and Auction Rooms are some of my favourite spots for coffee/brunch.
